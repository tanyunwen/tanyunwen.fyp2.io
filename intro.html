Block C Floor 2 Introduce

Nowadays, many university students are unaware of what classrooms have in academic buildings outside of their courses and what floor the classroom is on. Many university students will also not go to the course classroom floor outside their courses, such as not covering the course classroom by the floor location.

When university students need to go to a certain classroom, a certain facility, or a certain place need to collect things distributed, they must spend some or more time finding the location of the place and the facility. So, I think we need to improve university students’ understanding of the university and make it convenient and easier for students to find the floor.
